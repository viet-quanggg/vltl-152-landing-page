Image Requirements:
====================

1. logo.png (or logo.svg)
   - Recommended size: 150x80px
   - Format: PNG or SVG
   - Background: Transparent
   - Should contain your brand logo

2. hero-image.jpg
   - Recommended size: 1920x1080px or 1600x900px
   - Format: JPG
   - Subject: Healthcare/nursing scene, elderly care, or facility interior
   - Quality: High resolution
   - Example sources:
     * Unsplash.com
     * Pexels.com
     * Your own photography

3. team-1.jpg, team-2.jpg, team-3.jpg
   - Recommended size: 800x1000px (portrait orientation)
   - Format: JPG
   - Subject: Professional headshots or team photos
   - Should be consistent in style/quality
   - Aspect ratio: 4:5 (portrait)

4. Additional optional images:
   - Gallery images
   - Service icons
   - Background patterns
